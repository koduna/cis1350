History of the Website

The current history of the website: This website was created in the year 2015, in October. The website had two main reasons for being created. One, the website was meant as a memento to our wedding, and as a fun thing to look back on later on in life, maybe even show the kids and/or grandkids what we were like on our wedding day and just before. Two, the website actually will be graded by a teacher for certain requirements, and that to me will help out with making the website more complete and better formated.
